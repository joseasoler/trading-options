Customize your trading experience!

[img]https://i.imgur.com/grdjP2G.png[/img]

You can easily change:

• How frequently caravans, orbital traders or visitors will arrive at the colony.

• Change restock frequency of settlements.

• Change the departure time of caravans, visitors and orbital traders.

• Adjust the amount of silver in stock for any trader category.

• Modify the amount of other items in stock for any trader category.

• Make the amount of items and silver that traders have in stock scale with the wealth of your colony.

• Allow slaver traders to appear regardless of your colony population.

You can safely add Trading Options to an existing save-game. You can modify any of the settings at any point during the game. The changes will apply immediately, but only new traders will be affected.

[h2]Mod compatibility[/h2]

Trading Options is compatible with all mods that change the stock of a trader. The changes stack. Trading Options may be incompatible with any mod that changes trader frequency.

[h2]Frequently Asked Questions[/h2]

[b]Q:[/b] [i]Can I add this mod to an existing game?[/i]

[b]A:[/b] Yes.

-

[b]Q:[/b] [i]Can I modify any of the settings at any point during the game?[/i]

[b]A:[/b] Yes. The changes will apply immediately, but only new traders will be affected.

-

[b]Q:[/b] [i]How can I get the same amount of orbital traders as by [url=https://steamcommunity.com/sharedfiles/filedetails/?id=725465444]More Trade Ships[/url]?[/i]

[b]A:[/b] Set the "Orbital traders per period" slider to 1, and the "Period between orbital traders" slider to 4.

-

[b]Q:[/b] [i]How can I get the wealth same scaling as [url=https://steamcommunity.com/sharedfiles/filedetails/?id=1511375007]Supply and Demand[/url]?[/i]

[b]A:[/b] Set the "Wealth scaling" slider to "Loaded" for all trader categories.

-

[b]Q:[/b] [i]Is this mod compatible with [url=https://steamcommunity.com/workshop/filedetails/?id=2785616901]Vanilla Trading Expanded[/url]?[/i]

[b]A:[/b] Yes, both mods are fully compatible. Vanilla Trading Expanded can make stock prizes fluctuate, while Trading Options can change the amount of available stock. Both features can be together without any issues.

-

[b]Q:[/b] [i]Is this mod compatible with [url=https://steamcommunity.com/workshop/filedetails/?id=2890901044]Combat Extended[/url]?[/i]

[b]A:[/b] Combat Extended has the following documentation:
• [url=https://github.com/CombatExtended-Continued/CombatExtended/wiki/Frequently-Asked-Questions#does-mod-work-with-combat-extended-does-it-need-a-patch]Does (Mod) work with Combat Extended? Does it need a patch?[/url].
• [url=https://github.com/CombatExtended-Continued/CombatExtended/blob/Development/SupportedThirdPartyMods.md]Supported Third-Party Mods[/url].

I do not know enough about Combat Extended changes to guarantee if the mods are compatible or not. 

-

[b]Q:[/b] [i]Is this mod compatible with RimWorld 1.3?[/i]

[b]A:[/b] Trading Options is only compatible with 1.4. In 1.3 you can use [url=https://steamcommunity.com/sharedfiles/filedetails/?id=2813426619]Customizable Silver Stock[/url], [url=https://steamcommunity.com/sharedfiles/filedetails/?id=1511375007]Supply and Demand[/url] and [url=https://steamcommunity.com/sharedfiles/filedetails/?id=1369828656]Adjustable Trade Ships[/url] to get almost the same feature set.
